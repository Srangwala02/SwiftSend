    Email Configuration

    To configure your Gmail account to send email using Nodemailer, you need to enable "Less Secure Apps" and optionally enable 2-step verification.

    You have already configured your Gmail Account for the mail.

    How to do it ?
    1. Go to www.gmail.com and sign in to the Gmail account which you want to use to send emails.
    2. Click on the gear icon in the top right corner of the page and select "Settings" from the dropdown menu.
    3. Click on the Accounts and Import tab.
    4. Under the Change account settings section, click on the Other Google Account settings link.
    5. This will redirect you to the Manage Account Page. Click on Security tab in the left-hand menu.
    6. Under Signing in to Google. click on 2-Step Verification and follow the prompts to enable it.
    7. Turn on Allow less secure apps This will allow Nodemailer to access your Gmail account to send email.
    8. Once the 2-step verification is enabled, Click on App Passcode
